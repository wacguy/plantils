About

Plantils is developing legume and tuber crops that contain vitamin B12. Our mission is to encourage plant-based and cruelty-free, nutrition and promote healthier, ethical and environmentally conscious nutrition.

Team

Guy Wachsman

Founder

I am a plant biologist with 20 years of experience in development, genetics, bioinformatics and molecular biology.

I founded Plantils because I believe we must shift towards sustainable and ethical nutrition practices: eat more plant-based foods and spare the farm animals, our health and the environment.

Our goal at Plantils is to develop vitamin B12-fortified crops, providing consumers with animal-free and nourishing dietary choices.
